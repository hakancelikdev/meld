The rules

Four tiny rules.
A world of decisions.

Every Meld puzzle is a handful of tiles, a move budget, and one quiet goal — empty the board.

1 → 1
01

Slide

Swipe up, down, left or right. Every tile on the board moves in that direction until it hits something.

1 1 → 2
02

Merge

Two identical tiles that collide become one — with the next value. 1 + 1 = 2. 2 + 2 = 3. It keeps climbing.

4 → ·
03

Dissolve

A tile that reaches the dissolve value — 4 in most chapters — quietly vanishes from the board.

· · ·
04

Clear

Remove every tile before the move budget runs out. Finish at par for three stars. Every move counts.

Under the hood

Built for the way you actually play.

Play offline on the train. Pick up on another device. No logins, no friction, no nonsense.

900 levels, zero filler

150 hand-designed puzzles introduce every mechanic. 750 procedural levels — all verified solvable — push the rest.

A new puzzle every day

Same daily challenge worldwide, same weekly challenge everyone. Build streaks, earn rewards, compete globally.

Speedrun mode

Ten fresh levels. One race against the clock. Fastest run on the Game Center leaderboard takes the day.

Endless mode

Infinite procedural puzzles with rising difficulty. The run ends when you run out of moves — not before.

iCloud sync

Your progress, stars and purchases follow you across every device on your Apple ID. No account. No logins.

Five ways to play

One game.
Many rhythms.

Play a quick daily. Race a speedrun on your break. Sink an evening into the campaign. They all live in the same minimalist space.

  • Campaign — 900 levels

    12 chapters. 3×3 intros to 5×5 mastery. Frozen tiles, blockers, extended dissolves.

  • Daily Challenge

    A single shared puzzle each day. Keep your streak alive — use a freeze if you miss one.

  • Weekly Challenge

    Seven curated puzzles, one per day, with reward tiers for partial and perfect weeks.

  • Speedrun

    Ten levels, one timer. Post the fastest run on the global leaderboard.

  • Endless

    Procedural puzzles that ramp up forever. See how far you can push the streak.
